1. Setting Up Scales

Enter the Original Flute Scale your are holding (e.g., C) and the Flute Scale (e.g., A) to set up the scale transposition. This defines you want to play the A scale notes in the flute of scale C scale.

2. Entering Notes

Type your musical notes in the input area. Notes should be separated by dots or space. You can take the reference from the Notations World.

3. Viewing Results

After pressing the Convert Scale button, you'll see the transposed notes displayed as an output. Copy the notes to clipboard for further use!

4. Explore

Explore finger placements by clicking on the Go to Fingers placed after the output container.
